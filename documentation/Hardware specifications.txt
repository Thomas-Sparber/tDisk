tDisk Hardware specifications

Computer type:
 - Single board computer (SBC),
 - Computer-on-module (COM),
 - System on a Chip (SoC)
CPU:
 - 2-4 Cores
 - min 1.2 GHz
 - (32bit or 64bit)
 - (ARM or x86)
RAM:
 - min 1GB
 - recommended 2GB+
 - (DDR3 or DDR4)
Internal Storage:
 - Micro SD
 - NAND Flash
Other:
 - WLAN
 - 1GBit Ethernet
 - USB 2.0 Ports
 - USB 3.0 Ports
 - SATA Port

NOT NEEDED
 - Bluetooth
 - HDMI
 - VGA, DVI
 - GPU
 - Audio
